## Image Quality Guidelines

### Hero Background
- **Size:** 1920x1080px or larger (landscape)
- **Subject:** Beautiful spa interior, treatment room, or calming atmosphere
- **Quality:** High resolution, professional photography
- **Format:** JPG or WebP

### Service Thumbnails
- **Size:** 600x400px (3:2 aspect ratio)
- **Subject:** Service-related imagery or results
- **Quality:** Clear, well-lit photos
- **Format:** JPG or WebP

### Service Hero Images
- **Size:** 1200x600px (2:1 aspect ratio)
- **Subject:** Treatment in action or beautiful results
- **Quality:** Professional, high-resolution
- **Format:** JPG or WebP

### Before/After Images
- **Size:** 800x800px (square, 1:1 aspect ratio)
- **Subject:** Clear, well-lit client results
- **Quality:** Same lighting, angle, and distance for both before/after
- **Format:** JPG or WebP
- **Privacy:** Ensure client consent for all result photos

## Quick Placeholder Setup

1. Visit Unsplash, Pexels, or Pixabay
2. Search for relevant terms (see above)
3. Download high-quality images
4. Rename them to match the required filenames
5. Place them in `public/images/services/` or `public/images/results/`
6. Replace with real photos before going live

---

## Image Optimization

Before adding images to the site:

1. **Compress images** using:
   - TinyPNG (https://tinypng.com)
   - Squoosh (https://squoosh.app)
   - ImageOptim (Mac)

2. **Convert to WebP** for better performance (optional but recommended)

3. **Keep file sizes small:**
   - Hero images: under 500KB
   - Service images: under 200KB
   - Result images: under 150KB

---

## Adding More Before/After Images

To add multiple before/after photos for a service:

1. Add the image files to `public/images/results/`
2. Edit `data/services.ts`
3. Find the service you want to update
4. Add to the `beforeAfter` array:

```typescript
beforeAfter: [
  {
    before: '/images/results/service-before-1.jpg',
    after: '/images/results/service-after-1.jpg',
    caption: '8-week transformation',
  },
  {
    before: '/images/results/service-before-2.jpg',
    after: '/images/results/service-after-2.jpg',
    caption: '12-week transformation',
  },
  // Add more as needed
],
```
